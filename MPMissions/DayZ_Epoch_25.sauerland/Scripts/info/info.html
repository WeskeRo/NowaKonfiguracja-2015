------- Help and Information -------


Press HOME key for debug monitor.

Press O key for EAR PLUGS.

Right click on TOOLBOX, to deploy bike, motorbike, mozie.

Use RADIO for GROUP MANAGEMENT.

Use KOSTEY'S NOTEBOOK for FUN MENU.

Use EVIDENCE 1 to build VIRTUAL GARAGE.

Use EVIDENCE 2 to build Heli Pad for virtual garage (spawn point).

Right click a Bloodbag to Bloodbag yourself.

Right click a secondary weapon to commit suicide.

Right click a Knife near a hemp field to harvest it.

Right click binoculars to change your view distance.
